Know Before You Go: Returns are prohibited

Hobby Lobby strives to accommodate its customers' needs, but there are a few items that cannot be returned:

  • Machines from Cricut®, HeatPressNation, and Sewing Machines: These items cannot be returned or exchanged unless the customer presents the original receipt.
  • If you have a warranty claim, you must direct it to the manufacturer.
  • Hobby Lobby reserves the right to limit or refuse the return of specific products and items that have not been received.
